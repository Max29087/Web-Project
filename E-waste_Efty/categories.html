Recyclable E-Waste Categories

We accept various types of electronic waste for responsible recycling

Mobile Phones

Smartphones, feature phones, and accessories

Examples include:

  • Smartphones and feature phones
  • Phone chargers and cables
  • Phone cases with electronic parts

Laptops & Computers

Laptops, desktops, and computer peripherals

Examples include:

  • Laptops and notebooks
  • Desktop computers
  • Keyboards, mice, webcams

Batteries

Rechargeable and non-rechargeable batteries

Examples include:

  • Rechargeable batteries (Li-ion, NiMH)
  • Laptop and phone batteries
  • Power banks

Appliances

Small electronic appliances

Examples include:

  • Microwaves and toasters
  • Coffee makers and blenders
  • Electric kettles and irons

Tablets

iPads, Android tablets, and e-readers

Examples include:

  • iPads and Android tablets
  • E-readers (Kindle, Kobo)
  • Drawing tablets
monitor
40 pts

Monitors & TVs

Computer monitors and television sets

Examples include:

  • LCD and LED monitors
  • CRT monitors and TVs
  • Smart TVs and displays

Important Guidelines

  • Please remove all personal data before recycling devices
  • Remove SIM cards and memory cards from phones and tablets
  • Do not send items with physical damage that could be hazardous
  • Batteries should be taped at terminals to prevent short circuits
  • Pack items securely to prevent damage during collection
